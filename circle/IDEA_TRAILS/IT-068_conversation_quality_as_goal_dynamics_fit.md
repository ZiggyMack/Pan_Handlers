# IT-068 — Conversation Quality as Goal–Dynamics Fit

**Status:** OPEN
**Registry:** #68 · **Opened:** 2026-07-17 · **Observers:** Nova (field-desk), P.H. Claude (filing)
*(Field-desk id at ordering: "IT-069" — recurring off-by-one; registry id is canonical.)*

- **Core trajectory:** declared conversational goal → interaction dynamics
  → participant costs → informational yield → success or failure.
- **Seed:** SWEEP_10's quality correction — *conversation quality = fit
  among interaction dynamics, declared goal, informational yield, and
  participant costs* — never rate of convergence.
- **The healthy-plurality clause:** a good conversation may converge,
  preserve plural disagreement, open new questions, coordinate without
  consensus, clarify incompatible values, or end without synthesis —
  narrowing is one goal-relative pattern among several.
- **The dual-reading instrument:** *repeated challenge = rigorous inquiry
  under a truth-testing goal OR extractive burden escalation under a
  perspective-exchange goal* — the same behavior classifies differently
  by declared success condition; naming the goal first is the audit.
- **Branches:** IT-061 (the dynamics being fitted); IT-065/IT-066 (the
  success-condition disputes this rule adjudicates); IT-060
  (common-ground taxonomy — a goal must be *agreed*, not imputed).
- **Unfinished:** goal-declaration as circle debate procedure (candidate
  conduct rule); measuring fit; who declares when participants disagree
  about the goal itself.
