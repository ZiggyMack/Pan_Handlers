  pressed — what ultimately underwrites action under residual
  uncertainty? — Ziggy's trilemma offered God, brute fact, or
  ignorance. Is that fork exhaustive, or an artifact of how the
  question was posed?

  in-thread):** values, preferences, practical identity, constitutive
  norms, utility, habit, social commitments, biological disposition —
  ordinary termini that are neither God, nor brute fact, nor
  ignorance.
- **Binding restrictions:** no God/brute-fact/ignorance-as-exhaustive
  claim; no practical-underdetermination-to-God inference; the
  trilemma is a *widening* device (it reopens options), never a
  forcing one.
