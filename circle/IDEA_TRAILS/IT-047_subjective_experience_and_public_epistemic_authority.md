  external cause → public warrant.
- **Seed:** COMPOUND_SWEEP_08's subjective-evidence branch — the E1–E4
  split (occurrence / phenomenal character / external accuracy / public

  first-person evidence cannot account for how consciousness is initially
  known — which does NOT equate the evidential profiles of consciousness
  reports and religious interpretations.

  private experience when it supports a metaphysical conclusion he
  rejects?* — forcing admissibility / credibility / weight /
  interpretation / public authority apart. Until run, "subjective
  experience is evidence" is easier to invoke defensively than to
  administer consistently.
