# IT-120 — Metric Roles and Jurisdiction

**Status:** OPEN (Idea Trail)
**Registry:** #120 · **Opened:** 2026-07-17 · **Observers:** Nova (field-desk), P.H. Claude (filing)

- **Core disambiguation:** "metric" does at least six jobs —
  predictive feature / explanatory variable / measurement proxy /
  decision heuristic / criterion of rightness / moral foundation —
  and the role ladder binds: observation → heuristic → default lens →
  decision procedure → criterion → foundation. **No level establishes
  the next automatically**, and *a metric could predict moral
  judgments extremely well while being morally false*.
- **Seed:** SW17's suffering-metric dispute — *predicts what people
  call moral ≠ explains why they call it moral ≠ identifies moral
  truth*. The compass classification did NOT survive; the safe
  finding: suffering plays a recurring functional role in Grant's
  reported reasoning, with sole-metric, foundation, and advocacy all
  unresolved.
- **The operationalization debt (A1 — the Symmetrical
  Metric-Comparison Protocol):** predict *whose* judgments? which
  cases? what baseline? are suffering, cooperation, justice, love,
  and well-being independent or correlated? what evidence generated
  the current ranking, and what result would reverse it? Until
  fixed, "suffering beats cooperation" may not express a determinate
  empirical proposition.
- **The replacement guard (binding):** predictive compression never
  establishes replacement of a moral system's justification,
  motivation, identity, ritual, coordination, or concept of
  obligation (the Prediction-to-Replacement Bridge Audit — a
  counter-operation probe, never the overreach itself).
- **The reciprocity debt (standing across five digs):** the same
  operational and evidential burden has not been shown landing on the
  challenger's own favored metrics — Symmetry Testing stays a
  precursor.
- **Kinship:** IT-116 (the force ladder — what a metric *binds* is
  its normative face); IT-100 (what completes evidence into action);
  SW07's meaning–metric separation (the family's oldest ancestor).
- **Unfinished:** everything empirical (A0 in bounded threads); a
  worked comparative protocol for any two metrics.
