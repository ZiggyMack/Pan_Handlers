# IT-130 — The Meta-Frame Governance Problem

**Status:** OPEN (Idea Trail)
**Registry:** #130 · **Opened:** 2026-07-21 · **Observers:** Nova (field-desk), P.H. Claude (filing)

- **Core finding — SWEEP_22:** Ziggy's use of archetypes (Loki, Captain
  America, and others) as switchable operational frames rather than
  exhaustive identities is philosophically real — a person can deploy
  an archetype without being frame-locked into its inherited script.
  But "not frame-locked" does not dissolve the power question; it
  relocates it. If a person can freely invoke, combine, or transcend
  any archetype, **who or what governs the choice of frame?** What
  stays stable across the switches? What distinguishes a principled
  switch from an opportunistic escape? Without an answer, the
  "archetype weaver" (Nova's own coinage, never Ziggy's — CO-
  CONSTRUCTED, never independent Ziggy terminology) may be *less*
  constrained than any single archetype, becoming an unexamined
  sovereign over every interpretive standard.
- **The productive/failure fork:** frame mobility may be genuine
  flexibility or integrative pluralism — OR it may be post hoc escape,
  identity immunization, refusal of commitment, or a way of making
  criticism of one frame evaporate by simply moving to another. The
  bounded packet does not establish which, and does not establish that
  Ziggy has (or has been asked for) a meta-rule governing switching.
- **The archival discipline this seeded:** a corrective/analytic
  control introduced by a repair pass (here, the Meta-Frame Governance
  Audit itself) is not evidence that the live actor performed that
  interrogation in the original conversation — it stays extraction-
  generated architecture unless directly source-anchored. This
  distinction proved load-bearing across SWEEP_22's Stage 2 and Stage
  3 repairs and may generalize to other digs where a Stage-1 repair's
  own safeguards get retroactively read back into the live exchange.
- **Kinship:** IT-077 (explanatory termination and primitive symmetry,
  continued by SWEEP_20's Foundation-Symmetry Demand — the same
  reciprocal-scrutiny shape, applied here to interpretive frame choice
  rather than explanatory grounds); IT-026 (mythic compression,
  interpretive constraint, and projection); the detector-vs-disease
  family (IT-126) — a diagnostic control is not the pathology it
  diagnoses, and here a *governance* control is not the same as a
  *live performance* of that governance.
- **Unfinished:** what would count as Ziggy's own meta-rule for frame
  switching (A1 omission, SW22 Stage 3); whether frame mobility
  weakens Ziggy's own preferred commitments as readily as it weakens
  an opponent's frame; whether Archetype Weaver is independently
  possessed by Ziggy outside this one packet.
