# IT-053 — Miracles: Possibility, Probability, and Historical Evidence

**Status:** OPEN
**Registry:** #53 · **Opened:** 2026-07-17 · **Observers:** Nova (field-desk), P.H. Claude (filing)
*(Field-desk id at ordering: "IT-054" — recurring off-by-one; registry id is canonical.)*

- **Core trajectory:** logical possibility → metaphysical possibility →
  biological regularity → historical testimony → posterior confidence.
- **Seed:** COMPOUND_SWEEP_09's Modality–Evidence Audit — the five-modality
  ladder with both reciprocal rules: *ordinary biology does not establish
  metaphysical impossibility* AND *metaphysical possibility does not
  establish historical occurrence.*
- **The M1–M4 split carried:** prior history / miraculous conception /
  virginity in delivery / perpetual virginity — the bounded thread never
  froze which proposition was disputed; both sides may have "won" by
  answering different questions.
- **The biological-regularity concession (binding):** regularity cannot
  logically disprove an intervention claim, but it *legitimately affects
  the prior and comparative burden attached to testimony* — the clause
  that keeps category separation from becoming category exclusion.
- **The six-way status split (central repair):** metaphysically possible /
  nonzero probability / unknown probability / historically plausible /
  rationally permissible / rationally required — never interchangeable.
- **Eight-step historical-return procedure carried** (define claim →
  earliest sources → independence/genre → compare explanations → price
  regularity → price the intervention prior → explanatory fit →
  provisional confidence). None of its evidence is in the packet: *the
  thread analyzes the framing of plausibility; it does not establish the
  historical credibility or incredibility of Mary's virginity.*
- **Branches:** IT-057 (repair vs escape — what happens after the modal
  work); IT-058 (the reciprocal audit both frameworks owe); IT-016 (the
  Bell technical gate — precedent for claims needing expert evidence).
- **Unfinished:** everything historical; the rival-miracle symmetry test;
  what evidence would distinguish miracle from legend or theological
  development.
