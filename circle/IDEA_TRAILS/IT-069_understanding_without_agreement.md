# IT-069 — Understanding Without Agreement

**Status:** OPEN
**Registry:** #69 · **Opened:** 2026-07-17 · **Observers:** Nova (field-desk), P.H. Claude (filing)
*(Field-desk id at ordering: "IT-070" — recurring off-by-one; registry id is canonical.)*

- **Core trajectory:** position expressed → listener reconstructs it →
  speaker verifies the reconstruction → disagreement recorded → evidence
  tested → no conversion required.
- **Seed:** SWEEP_10's success-condition work distilled to a verification
  procedure — the reconstruction-check makes "you understand me"
  testable instead of vibes-based.
- **The permanent separations:** *you understand me ≠ you agree with me;
  you challenge me ≠ you failed to understand me; you remain unconvinced
  ≠ conversational failure.*
- **Kinship (cross-excavation):** the interpersonal face of the
  charitable-reconstruction discipline the archive already runs on
  sources (freeze a neutral reconstruction before scoring — SW08's
  Omission 1; SW09's P1–P4); here the same move becomes a *live
  conversational* protocol.
- **Branches:** IT-065 (the orientation this procedure serves); IT-051
  (what recorded disagreement obliges); DEBATES/ (candidate procedure for
  circle debates — reconstruct-verify-then-dispute).
- **Unfinished:** the procedure run in a live circle exchange; what
