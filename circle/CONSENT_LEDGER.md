## Scope Notes

- "Share ideas" covers: positions, arguments, reasoning operators, debate records,
  idea trails, and intellectual relationship dynamics.
- It does **not** cover sensitive-personal, non-reasoning material — which is
  excluded at ingestion anyway (`ETHICS_AND_CONSENT.md` §2), so no consent scope
  is ever asked to stretch over it.
- Consent to share ideas ≠ endorsement of the archive's *interpretations*.
  Members' rights of reply (`ETHICS_AND_CONSENT.md` §6) are a standing offer.
- A new scope (e.g., attributed publication beyond this repo, quotation in a
  paper) needs a new ledger row, not an assumption.
