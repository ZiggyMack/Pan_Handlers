## Scope Notes

- "Share ideas" covers: philosophical positions, arguments, reasoning operators,
  debate history, idea trails, project-relevant relational dynamics, and
  **bounded source excerpts** (direct quotes require reliable, recoverable
  source anchors — `ETHICS_AND_CONSENT.md` §4).
- It does **not** cover sensitive-personal, non-reasoning material — which is
  excluded at ingestion anyway (`ETHICS_AND_CONSENT.md` §2), so no consent scope
  is ever asked to stretch over it. Psychological diagnoses and alleged hidden
  motives are prohibited outright.
- Consent to share ideas ≠ endorsement of the archive's *interpretations*.
  Members' rights of reply (`ETHICS_AND_CONSENT.md` §6) are a standing offer;
  the response-mechanism column is where corrections land.
- A new scope (e.g., attributed publication beyond this repo, quotation in a
  paper) needs a new register row, not an assumption.
- The response mechanism for non-members whose words appear (e.g., AI
  collaborators like Nova) is attribution discipline: their formulations are
  labeled theirs and never converted into member claims.
